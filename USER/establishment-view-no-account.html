Overview

"Buns & Slices" is a fun and fast casual eatery serving up mouthwatering hotdogs, juicy burgers, and cheesy, oven-baked pizzas. Located in the heart of downtown, we pride ourselves on using fresh ingredients and crafting delicious meals that hit the spot every time. Whether you're grabbing a classic cheeseburger, a gourmet pizza with all the toppings, or a fully-loaded hotdog, we've got something for everyone.
